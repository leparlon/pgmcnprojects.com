PRIVACY POLICY

YET ANOTHER TINY ROGUELIKE

EFFECTIVE DATE: MARCH 2026

1. OVERVIEW

This Privacy Policy describes how PGMCN Projects ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the mobile application Yet Another Tiny Roguelike (the "App"), available on the Google Play Store.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This App is a single-player mobile game and does not require account registration, does not collect personal information, and does not transmit any data to our own servers.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data. The App does not request access to your contacts, camera, microphone, location, files, or any other sensitive device resources.

All game data (scores, progress, settings) is stored locally on your device.

3. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

The App integrates Google Play Games Services to provide optional features such as achievements and sign-in. If you choose to sign in with Google Play Games, Google may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

We have no access to, and do not control, the data collected by Google.

  • Google Play Games Services Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Play Terms of Service: play.google.com/about/play-terms

Use of Google Play Games Services is entirely optional. All core gameplay functions are available without signing in.

4. ANALYTICS & ADVERTISING

The App does not include any advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or tracking libraries of any kind. No usage data, crash reports, or behavioral analytics are collected by us.

5. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any personal data at all, the App is safe for all ages in that regard.

6. DATA SECURITY

Because we do not collect or transmit personal data, there is no user data stored on our end that could be subject to a security breach. Local game data is stored on your device and is governed by your device's own security model.

7. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
